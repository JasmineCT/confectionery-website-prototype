Our Chocolateiers

A chocolateier is a perosn or comapny who makes confectionary from chocolate. Our Chocolateiers are trained form all around the would in the art of heating and cooling of chocolate to create the desired characteristics like shininess of the chocolate or 'snap', the way it breaks. It is a technique we mastered over the years.
