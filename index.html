Our Chocolatiers

A chocolateier is a perosn or comapny who makes confectionery from chocolate. Our Chocolatiers are trained form all around the world in the art of heating and cooling of chocolate to create the desired characteristics like shininess of the chocolate or 'snap', the way it breaks. It is a technique we've mastered over the years.
